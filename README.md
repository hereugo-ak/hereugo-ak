### The Story

MBA student who refused to wait for a "technical co-founder." Started building AI products from scratch — learned Kotlin, Python, FastAPI, React, Ktor, and cloud infra by shipping real apps used by real people. No bootcamp. No CS degree. Just relentless building.

Built **Quantum Insights**, a multi-agent AI BI platform live in production with 15+ AI agents and 7 LLM providers. Now building **Enroll+**, a full-stack AI-powered school management platform serving Indian schools — single-handedly across Android, iOS, Web, and Desktop.
